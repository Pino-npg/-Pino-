🧭 Who is Pino?

A sensitive and curious creator, Pino is a free spirit with a deep connection to nature and animals. He loves traveling, exploring different cultures, and learning from every encounter.

To him, borders are just human inventions: what truly matters are values — not ethnicity, religion, or origin. His art aims to break down barriers, speak a shared language, and remind us that deep down we’re all part of the same humanity.
